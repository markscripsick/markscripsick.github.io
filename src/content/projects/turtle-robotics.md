## Overview

TURTLE Robotics is a student team at Texas A&M building competitive robots. As
**team lead**, I own the technical direction and keep three workstreams in sync:
mechanical design, electronics, and software.

## My role

- **Mechanical CAD:** led the SolidWorks assembly for the chassis and key
  subsystems, focusing on manufacturability and serviceability.
- **Circuit integration:** coordinated power distribution, motor controllers,
  and sensor wiring with the electronics sub-team.
- **Software coordination:** aligned the controls/software team's interfaces with
  the hardware so subsystems came together cleanly.
- **Leadership:** ran build sprints, set milestones, and unblocked the team.

## Highlights

- Took the robot from **concept to a working, tested system** within the build
  season.
- Standardized the CAD workflow so subsystem owners could work in parallel
  without breaking the master assembly.
